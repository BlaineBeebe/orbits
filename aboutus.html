Who We Are

We maintain, support your IT ASSETS needs, and fully recycle any E Waste based on our re-use/repair/resale ideology.

ORB-IT

ORB-IT is a minority-owned business. While our mission remains improving the planet’s E-Waste concern, we strive to create work opportunities for the underrepresented population, minorities and those with disability. At present we work with several agencies that aids individuals with disabilities in futherane of our goals.

We live in a society where technological innovation is inevitable and our reliance on those devices is on an upward incline. As such, it is imperative that we do better in managing that waste. From managing existent assets to its fullest potential. And leading the industry in specific E-Waste management.
