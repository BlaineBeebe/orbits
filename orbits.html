Global E-Waste

In 2019, US alone produced 6.92 million tons of E-Waste which roughly amount to 46 lbs per person. Globally the E-Waste output is currently weighed equivalent of 375 cruise ships. The scale of the problem grew by 21% from 2014-2019.
